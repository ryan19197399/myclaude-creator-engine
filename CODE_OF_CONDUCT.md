# Code of Conduct

This project exists because people with different skills — developers, consultants, researchers, writers, marketers — are building tools together. That diversity is a strength, not a challenge to manage.

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Focus on what is best for the community and ecosystem
- Accept feedback gracefully and offer it kindly
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Sustained disruption of discussions or contributions
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other community channel.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to conduct@myclaude.sh. All reports will be reviewed and responded to promptly. Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code.
